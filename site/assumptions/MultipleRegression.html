Assumptions: Multiple Regression

  1. Independence: The residuals are statistically independent.
  2. Normality: The residuals follows a Normal distribution.
  3. Homoscedasticity (Homogeneity of Variance): The variance of the residuals has a constant variance (at each level of the predictors).
  4. Unbiasedness (linearity): The mean of the residuals is 0 (at each level of the predictors).
